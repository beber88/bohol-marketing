## 7 Winning Patterns From Top-Performing Real Estate Ads

### Pattern 1: Lifestyle-First, Specs-Second
**Finding:** Ads that lead with "imagine your life here" outperform feature lists by 35-42%.
**Application:**
- IL: Open with "ליווי ישראלי מהשקל הראשון" not property specs
- PH: Open with "Your property works while you sleep" not PHP amounts
- Use sunset/golden hour imagery, not floor plans
- Show people enjoying the space, not empty rooms

### Pattern 2: Video Dominates
**Finding:** Video walkthroughs get 42% more engagement than static images. Agent/narrator presence builds trust.
**Application:**
- 15-second Reels for awareness (we have scripts ready)
- Use Nano Banana 2 to generate cinematic stills as video thumbnails
- Drone footage opener (3 seconds) then interior reveal
- Captions mandatory (80%+ watch on mute)

### Pattern 3: Comparison Anchoring
**Finding:** Ads showing "price of X here = price of Y there" drive highest click-through for international investors.
**Application:**
- IL: "1,450,000 shekel = penthouse in Bohol. Same money = 3-room apartment in Tel Aviv"
- PH: "PHP 32.5M villa = PHP 395K monthly income. Better than a condo in BGC that sits empty"
- Split-screen visuals showing the contrast

### Pattern 4: Single Clear CTA
**Finding:** Ads with one CTA outperform multi-CTA ads by 25%.
**Application:**
- Awareness: "Learn More" (link to landing page)
- Consideration: "Get Investment Report" (lead magnet)
- Conversion: "WhatsApp Now" (direct message)
- Never mix CTAs in one ad

### Pattern 5: Social Proof + Numbers
**Finding:** Specific numbers build trust. "70% appreciation" outperforms "great investment."
**Application:**
- Always lead with a number: 395,000, 17-25%, 65%, 60 seconds
- "Israeli investors already earning" (without fake testimonials)
- "Verified Airbnb data" as trust signal
- Show occupancy rates, not vague promises

### Pattern 6: Carousel for Consideration
**Finding:** Carousel ads get 3x more clicks than single-image for property showcases.
**Application:**
- Card 1: Aerial/lifestyle hero shot
- Card 2: Pool + jacuzzi
- Card 3: Interior luxury details
- Card 4: Location map (between JW Marriott and Mithi)
- Card 5: Income/ROI summary with CTA

### Pattern 7: Urgency Without Desperation
**Finding:** "2 units remaining" + time-bound offers work. "LAST CHANCE" does not.
**Application:**
- "2 villas available. When they close, they close."
- "Current pricing valid through [date]"
- Not: "BUY NOW OR MISS OUT FOREVER"

| Conversion | Golden hour urgency | 2 variations | FB Feed, Reels |
| Conversion | Exclusive/last-chance | 2 variations | FB Feed |

**Total: ~40 creatives per market = ~80 total**

### Prompt Engineering Rules

1. Always include "No text overlays" - text goes in the ad copy, not burnt into images
2. Reference actual property renders to maintain architectural accuracy
3. Specify "professional real estate photography" to avoid AI-looking outputs
4. Include lighting direction (golden hour, warm tones)
5. Specify placement aspect ratio in every prompt

### Post-Generation Workflow

1. Generate base images with Nano Banana 2
2. Review for quality and accuracy
3. Add text overlays using Meta's built-in tools (not burned in)
4. A/B test: AI-generated vs actual property renders
5. Best performers get scaled budget

## Ad Copy Improvements (Based on Research)

### Current Copy: Strong Points
- Consistent numbers (PHP 395K, 17-25% ROI)
- Scarcity messaging (2 units)
- Location anchoring (between 5-star hotels)
- Market-appropriate language

### Suggested Enhancements

**Israeli Market:**
1. Add comparison anchoring: "1,450,000 shekel. Less than a parking spot in north Tel Aviv."
2. Strengthen peer proof: "Israelis who invest in Southeast Asia see 17-25% returns while the Israeli market stagnates."
3. Simplify the legal structure mention - currently feels like a legal document

**Philippine Market:**
1. Lead with the "passive income while sleeping" angle more aggressively
2. Add BDO financing earlier in the copy (currently buried)
3. Use more Tagalog in awareness (higher engagement with local audience)
4. The "booking guests pay PHP 14K/night" stat is powerful - make it the opener
