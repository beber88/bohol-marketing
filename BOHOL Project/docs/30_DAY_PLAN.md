## OPTIMIZATION RULES (Always Active)

### Ad Kill Criteria
- CPM above $18: Kill immediately
- CTR below 0.8%: Kill immediately
- CPM above $15 (hooks): Kill after 3 days
- CTR below 1.5% (hooks): Kill after 3 days
- No leads after $100 spent on any ad set: Kill and restructure

### Ad Scale Criteria
- CTR above 3%: Double budget
- CPL below $30: Double budget
- Hook CTR above 3% or CPL below $30: Scale

### Budget Shift Rules
- If one market's CPL is 2x the other: shift 30% of budget
- If Google outperforms Meta on CPL: shift up to $10/day from Meta to Google
- Never reduce a performing campaign to fund a failing one

### Creative Refresh
- Replace any creative running 14+ days
- Always have 2 hooks per ad set (A/B)
- Never change image and copy simultaneously
