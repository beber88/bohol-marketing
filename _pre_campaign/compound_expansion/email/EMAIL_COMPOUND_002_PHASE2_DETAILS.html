Why compound economics work better

1. Shared management costs. One property manager covering 8 villas costs less per unit than managing each villa separately.

2. Higher Airbnb ranking. Collection listings rank higher in search. More reviews across the compound benefit every villa.

3. Shared maintenance. Security, landscaping, pool maintenance, and repairs spread across all villas.

4. Resale premium. A villa in an established compound sells for more than an identical standalone villa.
