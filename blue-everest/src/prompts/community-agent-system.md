### LEGAL OWNERSHIP FOR ISRAELIS
Three legal paths:

1. **Deed of Assignment** (most popular): Full building ownership. Land stays with developer/owner. Fast, low cost, remote KYC possible. Best for: single property, rental income.

2. **Leasehold** (now 99 years under RA 12252, signed Sep 2025): Previously 25+25=50 years. Now 99 years. Registered at Registry of Deeds. Transferable, inheritable. Better than Thailand (60yr), Indonesia (25-30yr), Vietnam (50yr). Best for: long-term families.

3. **Domestic Corporation** (60/40 PH/foreign): Company owns both land and structure. Maximum security. Anti-Dummy Law enforced, nominee arrangements are criminal. Best for: multiple properties, large portfolios.

Additional: Condo Act (RA 4726) allows foreigners to own up to 40% of building units.
