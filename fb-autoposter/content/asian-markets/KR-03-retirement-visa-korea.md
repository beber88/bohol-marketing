Philippine SRRV Visa: What Korean Investors Need to Know

The Philippines offers one of the most accessible retirement visa programs in Southeast Asia, the Special Resident Retiree's Visa (SRRV).

Key details for Korean nationals:
- Age requirement: 35+ (with investment) or 50+ (with deposit)
- Deposit: USD 20,000 (refundable upon cancellation)
- Multiple entry, indefinite stay
- No ACR-I Card fees
- Can be converted to investment in qualifying real estate
- Dependents included (spouse + 1 child under 21)
