Panglao vs Boracay: Why smart investors are looking south

Boracay is established. Everyone knows it. But here is why Panglao, Bohol, Philippines might be the better investment in 2026:

Price per sqm:
- Boracay: PHP 55,000-70,000/sqm
- Panglao: PHP 27,500-49,000/sqm
- Advantage: Panglao (30-50% cheaper entry)

Building regulations:
- Boracay: Strict limits since 2018 environmental closure. Height restrictions, setback requirements, limited new supply.
- Panglao: Development-friendly, growing supply of quality properties. Local government actively attracting investment.

Airport:
- Boracay (Caticlan): Small regional airport, limited capacity
- Panglao: International airport processed 2.22 million passengers in 2025, expanding routes to Korea, Japan, China

Tourism momentum:
- Boracay: Mature market, plateauing
- Bohol: 1.4 million tourists in 2025 (4.18% growth year-over-year), South Korea is top foreign source, Japan charter flights started May 2026

Projected Airbnb yields (with professional management):
- Boracay: 8-12% (competitive market, many operators)
- Panglao premium villas: Projected 14-20% gross yield (the average Panglao Airbnb runs 29% occupancy at $68/night - premium properties target 65%+ with professional management)

A practical example:
PHP 32,500,000 in Boracay buys you a small lot or a studio unit.
PHP 32,500,000 in Panglao buys you a 263.78 sqm luxury villa with 4 bedrooms, private pool, and a projected monthly income of PHP 395,000 with professional management.
