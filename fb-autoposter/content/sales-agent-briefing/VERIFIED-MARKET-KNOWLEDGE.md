## LEGAL FRAMEWORK (verified)

### RA 12252 - 99-Year Leasehold
- Signed: September 3, 2025
- Replaces: old 50+25 year limit
- Allows: foreign investors to lease private land for up to 99 years
- Transferable: yes, can be assigned, transferred, and used as collateral
- Source: Official Gazette, Philippine law

### Israel-Philippines Tax Treaty
- Signed: 1992
- Effective: January 1, 1997
- Purpose: prevention of double taxation
- Capital gains in PH: 6%
- Capital gains in Israel: 25%
- Source: Israeli Tax Authority, BIR

### Three Ownership Structures
1. **Deed of Assignment** - Most common. Transfer of rights to the structure (not land). Fast, simple.
2. **99-Year Leasehold** - Under RA 12252. Registered with Registry of Deeds. Transferable.
3. **Domestic Corporation** - 60% Filipino / 40% foreign. Can own land and structure. Best for multiple properties.
