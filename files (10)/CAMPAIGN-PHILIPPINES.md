## CAMPAIGN STRATEGY - PHILIPPINES

### Core Insight
Filipino high-income buyers are NOT motivated by the same things as foreign investors.
They respond to:
1. STATUS - owning a premium asset where their peers vacation
2. PASSIVE INCOME - "parang hindi ka nagtatrabaho"
3. FAMILY - "pang-anak, pang-apo na ito"
4. PRIDE - owning something better than a condo in BGC

The ROI angle works but must be secondary to lifestyle and status.
The scarcity angle works strongly - Filipinos respond to "mabibili na ng iba".

### Target Profile
- Location: Metro Manila (BGC, Makati, Alabang, Ortigas), Cebu City
- Age: 35-58
- Income: PHP 500,000+/month household
- Profile: Business owners, C-suite executives, OFW returnees with capital, real estate investors, doctors/lawyers with surplus income
- Behavior: Travels domestically frequently, considers Bohol/Boracay/Siargao

### Key Message for Filipinos
"Stop renting a villa in Bohol for PHP 50,000 a night. Own one."
