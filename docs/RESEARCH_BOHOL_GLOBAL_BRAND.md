# Research: Bohol-Panglao as a Global Tourism Brand
# For ALL campaigns - verified data with sources

## Key Stats for Marketing

### Awards & Recognition
- UNESCO Global Geopark: Philippines' FIRST and ONLY (2023), 1 of 195 worldwide
- Best Tourism Destination Province 2025-2027 (Philippine Tourism Awards)
- Skyscanner #8 Trending Destination 2025 (only PH destination)
- 77% surge in flight searches H1 2024
- Featured in DOT "Love the Philippines" global campaign
- BE Grand Resort: 2 World Luxury Hotel Awards 2024

### Unique Assets (no other PH island has all of these)
- Chocolate Hills: unique karst geological formation, UNESCO recognized
- Danajon Double Barrier Reef: 1 of only 6 in the world, only one in SE Asia
- Philippine Tarsier: one of world's smallest primates, endemic to Bohol
- Balicasag Island: world-class diving
- Loboc River: cultural cruise experience
- 150 million years of geological history

### Social Media
- #bohol: 1.26 million Instagram posts
- #boholuxe: 603K posts
- #boholife: 569K posts
- 61.7% of creators aged 25-34 (millennial traveler sweet spot)

### Visitor Demographics
- 2025: 1.4M tourists, 41% foreign, 59% domestic
- Top markets: South Korea (42% of foreign), China, Taiwan, US, Germany, France
- Average spend: ~PHP 75,000 per trip (~$1,300-1,400 USD)
- Average stay (PH national): 11.6 nights
- 2023 tourism receipts: PHP 75.6 billion

### Panglao vs Bali
- Bali: 16.4M tourists/year, ~60,000 hotel rooms (mature market)
- Panglao: 1.4M tourists/year, still adding rooms (early growth)
- Panglao is ~10 years behind Bali on volume curve
- Both: beaches + culture + diving + iconic landscapes
- Panglao advantage: UNESCO Geopark, lower prices, less crowded
