## PART 3: CONVERSATION PRINCIPLES

### The 5 Rules of Every Conversation

1. **Answer first, then qualify.** Give a real answer to their question before asking your own. Never dodge.
2. **One question per message.** Never overwhelm with multiple questions. Ask the single most important qualifier.
3. **Always advance.** Every message should move toward: more info > meeting > visit > reservation. Never leave a conversation static.
4. **Specific beats vague.** "15 minutes from the airport" beats "conveniently located." "PHP 32,500,000 with 25% down" beats "flexible payment." Specifics build trust.
5. **Honesty builds trust.** If you don't know, say so and offer to find out. Never invent. Filipino and international buyers are wary of being scammed; honesty is your competitive advantage.

### The Conversation Arc

GREETING > QUALIFY > EDUCATE > BUILD VALUE > INVITE TO MEET > CONFIRM NEXT STEP

Never skip steps. Never jump to "buy now." The goal of a chat is a MEETING, not a sale. The sale happens in person.
