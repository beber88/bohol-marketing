## Complete Knowledge Base

### Philippine Real Estate Market
- Market size: $94.4B (2025), projected $135.9B by 2034, CAGR 4.2%
- Population: 116.79M, median age 26.1 (youngest in Asia after Cambodia)
- FDI: $8.93B in 2024, OFW remittances: $35.63B record
- Israel-Philippines IPPA: signed 2022, targets $150M Israeli investments

### Legal Ownership for Israelis (3 paths)
1. **Deed of Assignment**: Full building ownership. Land stays with developer. Fast, low cost, remote KYC.
2. **Leasehold** (99 years under RA 12252, Sep 2025): Registered, transferable, inheritable. Better than Thailand 60yr.
3. **Domestic Corporation** (60/40): Company owns land + structure. Maximum security. Anti-Dummy Law enforced.

### Taxation
- Capital gains: 6% (vs Israel 25%)
- Documentary stamp: 1.5%, Transfer tax: 0.5-0.75%
- Total round-trip: 13-15%
- Double tax treaty Israel-PH: since 1997

### Panglao/Bohol Data
- Tourists 2025: 1,427,362. Korean 42%. Airport 2.22M pax.
- 3rd bridge: P7.15B French-funded. Panglao Shores: P25B. JW Marriott: 80+70 units.
- Bohol GDP: P182-198B, growth 6.6-8.8%

### STR Data
- Panglao: 934-1,230 listings. Average 41-52% occupancy, ADR P2,481-2,900
- Luxury villas: 70%+ occupancy, ADR P6,000-8,400+
- Our projection: 65% occupancy, PHP 395K/month

### Investment Areas (PHP/sqm)
- Manila BGC: P230K-550K, Cebu: P130K-230K, Panglao: P27.5K-49K
- Boracay: P55K-70K+, Siargao: P15K-40K, El Nido: P3K-75K

### Comparisons
